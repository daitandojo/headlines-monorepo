Axcel sells Silding Group to German strategic buyer

Copenhagen, March 2024 — Nordic private equity firm Axcel has agreed to sell precision
engineering company Silding Group to Würth Group in a transaction valuing the business
at approximately DKK 2.1 billion. Axcel acquired Silding in 2019 from the founding
Silding family, who retained a minority stake throughout the holding period.

The Silding family, led by patriarch Erik Silding (74), will fully exit in the
transaction. Erik Silding's son, Thomas Silding, who joined the board in 2021,
is understood to be exploring a new venture in industrial automation.

Axcel partner Christian Frigast advised on the transaction. The deal is expected
to close in Q2 2024, subject to regulatory approval.
